Dachshund Dog

Dog

The dachshund known as the wiener dog is a short-legged, long-bodied, hound-type dog breed.

  • Life expectancy:From 12 to 16 years old
  • Body Mass: 7.3 -15 kg (Standard size)
  • Origin: Germany
  • Colors: Black, Black and cinnamon, Chocolate and reddish, Blue and fire, Cream, Cinnamon, Chocolate and cream, Red

They may be smooth-haired, wire-haired, or long-haired. The standard -size dachshund was developed to scent, chase, and flush out badgers and other burrow- dwelling animals, while the miniature dachshund was bred to hunt smaller prey such as rabbits.
